criSys


Overview

criSys was born at IBM's Call for Code hackathon, hosted at UC Berkeley on April 26th-28th, 2019. This hackathon specifically was looking for solutions to help with community preparedness and resiliency to disasters.

Goal

Our IBM group - Brian, Felix, Paul, and Larry Wu - wanted to target communication in improving community resiliency during a disaster, which is why we came up with criSys.
